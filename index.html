Hello, I'm Alexandros, a passionate Multimedia Designer with a deep passion for modern fabrication techniques such as 3D Printing and Laser Cutting. My expertise lies
in 3D Modeling for physical prototyping and Arduino programming, which enables me to bring digital designs to life in captivating ways.

Additionally, I possess skills in video and sound production, allowing me to create immersive multimedia experiences that engage the senses.

I am also familiar with the powerful Unity game engine, which opens up a whole new realm of possibilities. With Unity, I can craft mesmerizing 2D, 3D, and virtual reality (VR) gaming experiences. By blending creativity and technology,

I have the ability to transform ideas into tangible wonders and create visually stunning and interactive content.
